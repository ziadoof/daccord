The DispoDeal service provided to users differs depending on the nature of the user and the state of their connection to the site or application.


2.2.1 - Visitor


Features that visitors can access from the website and the application:

• Access to all advertisements published on the site or application.

• The ability to search for an offer, a demand, a voyage, a meeting or an hosting.

• See the means of communication of the advertisers who wish it.

• Register as a user of the site or the application.

• Convert the site language between English and French.

2.2.2 - - User


Features that users can access from the website and the application:


• All the visitor access features of article 2.2.1.

• Log in to the user's account.

• Modification of personal data except e-mail, mandatory information: first name, e-mail, password, city.

Optional information: date of birth, gender, phone number, etc.

• Registration as a Carpool, Driver, Host.

• Activate and deactivate the status of Driver and / or Hosting.

• Allow or deny other users and visitors access to the user's contact information (not allowed by default)

• Determine the maximum distance of the radius of the activity circle (by default 10 km).

• Change the profile picture as User, Driver, Hosting, Carpool.

• Change the account password.

• Add and delete an Offer announcement.

• Add and delete a Demand announcement.

• Edit the title, description and price in the advertisements (Offer and demand).

• Access to all user announcements.

• Obtain suggested deals when their offer or demand matches the offer or demand of other users.

• The possibility of canceling and deleting the proposed deal.

• The possibility of sending messages to the second (or third) part of the deal.

• The possibility of accepting the deal.

• The possibility of sending a request to a Driver if the declared user is the owner of the demand.

• The possibility of adding the Driver to the deal.

• The possibility of evaluating the Driver after the end of the deal.

• The possibility of confirming the expiration of the deal.

• Get extra points after the deal ends.

• The possibility of adding a coffee request.

• The ability to see all coffee orders within a radius of 2 km

• The ability to send a message to the owner of a coffee request.

• The ability to receive messages for the coffee request submitted by the user.

• The possibility of canceling and deleting the coffee request.

• The ability to send hosting requests to other users.

• The possibility of following up the hosting request until it is processed.

• The possibility of confirming the end of the hosting process.

• The possibility of leaving a notice for the host after confirming the end of hosting.

• The possibility of knowing if the host has confirmed that the hosting has expired.

• Keep a copy of the hosting process.

• The possibility of adding an event.

• The ability to add an image to the event created by the user.

• Access to all events created by the user.

• The ability to manage the participant list and queue and remove and add users from the lists to

the appropriate list created by the user.

• The ability to comment on or remove the comment to the event.

• The ability to delete comments on the event created by the user.

• The ability to delete the user-generated event.

• the possibility of accessing all the events in which the user requested to participate or

already participated.

• The ability to access events geographically close to the user.

• The possibility of adding an evaluation to an event in which the user has participated.

• The ability to see all voyage to which the user has submitted a request to participate or in which he

has participated.

• The possibility of knowing the passengers participating in the voyage before participating.

• The ability to withdraw from a voyage that has been added as a participant.

• The ability to add all kinds of announcements to favorites.

• The ability to access favorites.

• Receive various notifications regarding transactions made by DispoDeal or other users.

• Receive messages from other users.

• Send messages to other users.

• Log out of the account.



2.2.2.1 – Driver


If the user is registered as a Driver:

• Receive delivery requests from other users.

• Accept or reject delivery requests.

• Confirm that the deal he was part of as Driver has expired.

• Obtain an evaluation as a Driver by the deal parties.

• Keep a copy of each delivery made.

• Obtain additional points as a Driver after each delivery.

2.2.2.2 – Host


If the user is registered as a host:


• Receive hosting requests from other users.

• The possibility of accepting or refusing accommodation requests.

• The possibility of confirming the end of the hosting process.

• Receive guest feedback after confirming hosting is complete.

• Earn additional points as a host from the guest after the hosting process is complete.

• Keep a copy of the hosting process.


2.2.2.3 - Carpool


If the user is registered as a carpool:


• Receive requests to participate in the voyage.

• The possibility of accepting or rejecting requests to participate.

• Add users with requests to the voyage.

• Cancel the user's participation in the voyage.

• Send and receive messages to request owners or trip participants.

• Obtain a participant evaluation after the voyage.

• Cancellation and cancellation of the voyage.

• Keep a copy of the voyage.
